Understand Your Addresses

Midnight uses 3 address types for privacy

NIGHT Address

Public
  • Holds NIGHT tokens (governance)
  • Visible on blockchain explorer
  • Generates DUST continuously

Shield Address

Private
  • Holds DUST tokens
  • Hidden from blockchain
  • Private transactions

DUST Recipient

Capacity
  • Receives generated DUST
  • Cap based on NIGHT amount
  • Decays if disconnected
